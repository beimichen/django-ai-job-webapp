About JobAssistant

JobAssistant is your friendly Artificial Intelligence-powered assistant, ready to help you generate professional, personalised resumes and cover letters so you don't have to stress out about job applications.

Interacting with JobAssistant is as simple as a having short chat. JobAssistant will do all the tedious work of analyzing what's best to include in your cover letter and resume with the support of industry wide insights and big data analysis.

As JobAssistant is powered by Machine Learning, the more you chat with JobAssistant, the smarter and more effective it becomes. Ultimately, JobAssistant improves your chances of being hired.
